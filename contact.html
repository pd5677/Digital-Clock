Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Digital Clock free to use?

Yes! Our Digital Clock service is completely free to use. All features including world clock, stopwatch, timer, and alarms are available at no cost.

How accurate is the time displayed?

The time accuracy depends on your device's system clock. We synchronize with your device's time, which is typically very accurate when connected to the internet.

Do alarms work when the browser is closed?

No, alarms only work while the browser tab is open. For reliable alarms, keep the tab open or use your device's built-in alarm system.

Can I add more cities to the world clock?

Currently, we display major cities. We're considering adding customizable city selection in future updates. Let us know which cities you'd like to see!

Why isn't my alarm working?

Make sure your browser allows sound notifications and that the tab remains open. Some browsers may block audio without user interaction.

Can I use this offline?

Basic clock functionality works offline once loaded, but world clock features require an internet connection for accurate time zone data.
